## 💡 About Me

I'm a **Backend-focused Software Developer** based in Istanbul, Turkey, with over **6 years of experience** in system design and infrastructure management. My journey started with JavaScript/Node.js and expanded into the .NET (C#) and PHP/Laravel ecosystems. I don't just build applications; I focus on how they are deployed, how they scale, and how they perform under high traffic.

- 🎓 Currently pursuing a B.S. in **Management Information Systems** at Istanbul University (Expected 2027), previously studied Information and Records Management at Istanbul Medeniyet University.
- 🔭 Focusing on **API Development, Database Design, CI/CD Automation, and Redis-based Performance Optimizations**.
- 🌱 Passionate about **Docker containerization**, establishing standard development environments, and simplifying operations with queue systems (Laravel Horizon).
- 💬 Ask me about **Node.js, Laravel, .NET, System Architecture, or DevOps pipelines**.
- ⚡ **Goal:** Building systems that make technical processes faster, more reliable, and highly sustainable for corporate workflows.
